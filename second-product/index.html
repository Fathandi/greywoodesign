First Meeting Starts in a Meaningful Living Room

The interior design of this living room was designed to bring a modern, spacious and cozy feel to the space.

By combining open architectural elements, natural lighting from large windows, and elegant custom furniture, this room becomes the center of family interaction as well as a representative area for entertaining guests.

The focus on color harmony, soft textures, and functional layout make it perfect for the modern urban lifestyle.

Stucture Architecture

The 4x6-meter room uses 60x60 cm polished granite flooring, warm ash doff paint finish walls, wood motif PVC suspended ceiling with LED ambient.

Synthetic leather custom L sofa, natural finish teak wood coffee table, MDF built-in TV shelf, and soft fur carpet. The 1.8x2.2m tempered glass window maximizes natural lighting.

Air circulation is supported by a hidden exhaust fan and an open space layout with lightweight partitions.
